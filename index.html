FIXED TERM: Spacious, 2 bedroom, unfurnished 2nd floor property for short let available NOW

Flat - Edinburgh

REF: 1455L: MONTPELIER PARK

 

SHORT LET: Spacious 2 bedroom property located in Montpelier Park, which is a quiet residential street situated in the very desirable area of Merchiston.  The local area benefits from a wide range of cafes, bars, restaurants and boutiques, and The Meadows, Tollcross, and Morningside are all within a short walking distance.  There are also regular bus links servicing the City Centre and surrounding areas, and the location offers easy transport links to the City Bypass.

 

The UNFURNISHED property benefits from many original period features and for additional cost MAY offer flexible furnishings for the right tenant. It comprises:

~Very large, spacious lounge with huge bay window, feature fireplace and wooden flooring

~Sizeable, bright kitchen with ample dining space, modern appliances and utility cupboard

~Master bedroom with carpeted flooring and feature fireplace

~Second double bedroom with carpeted flooring and feature fireplace

~Tiled bathroom with three-piece suite

~Entrance hallway offering storage availability

~Zone permit parking

~Gas heating

~Secure entry

~Pets considered (terms apply)

~Council tax: band E

~EPC rating: D

~Landlord registration: Pending

~Please note the property is due to be refurbished in 2018 and therefore only a three month lease is available

 

Available: NOW

(Lease for three months only; fixed) please note the property is due to be completely refurbished in 3-5 months’ time therefore a lease is only available for three months which may be extendable at a later date
